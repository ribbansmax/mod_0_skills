Class: Waiter

Attributes:
1. name (string)
2. tips (float)
3. num_tables (integer)
4. on_break (boolean)
5. schedule (array of strings)

Methods:
1. seat (This method gives the Waiter a table to serve. This modifies the num_tables attribute.)
2. break (This method sends the Waiter on a break for a set amount of time. This modifies the on_break attribute to 'true'.)
3. clear (This method has a table pay and leave. This modifies the tips and the num_tables.)
4. add_shift (This method adds a shift to the schedule array of the Waiter)
